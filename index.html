Welcome To TechQC

TechQC is an online support group created by the students of PSG Tech, to render a helping hand for those who are affected by sexual harassment and to create positive awareness about the LGBTQ community.

Who Are We

TechQC members and their volunteers are the students, alumnae and peers of PSG Tech joined hands with similar organizations all over India promoting and leading as a part of LGBTQ community.

Our Mission

The goals of every such LGBTQ community supporting pillars are to lend hand to help for the affected ones through sexual and other such actions and create positive awareness about the LGBTQ community not only within campus but for the ones who reach such communities even.

What We Do

  • Awareness campaigns about LGBTQ community
  • Support to the one who are the victims of sexual harassment
  • Online support
  • Discussions and forums about various ideologies supporting and against LGBTQ community
